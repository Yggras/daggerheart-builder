# ADR-0002: Project Scope

Status: Accepted
Date: 2026-05-24

## Context

The desired product is a Daggerheart companion app inspired by D&D Beyond, with a compendium, character builder, and playable campaign view.

## Decision

The project will initially target personal use and focus on three major product areas:

1. A searchable SRD compendium.
2. A wizard-like character builder.
3. A campaign play view with character sheets, DM tools, and real-time updates.

The app should target iOS, Android, and web.

## Consequences

- Product design must support mobile and web from the start.
- The SRD data foundation should be built before advanced app features.
- Public release, marketplace functionality, and commercial distribution are out of scope for now.

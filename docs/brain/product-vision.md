# Product Vision

## Summary

Create a personal-use Daggerheart companion app inspired by the utility of D&D Beyond, focused on the official Daggerheart SRD and designed for iOS, Android, and web.

The app has three major product areas:

1. Compendium
2. Wizard-like character builder
3. Playable campaign view with character sheets, DM view, and real-time updates

## Goals

- Provide a searchable, structured compendium for SRD rules content.
- Let users build Daggerheart characters step by step.
- Support playable campaign sessions with real-time shared state.
- Store user-owned characters and campaigns under user accounts.
- Work offline where possible, especially for compendium and character sheet use.
- Preserve original SRD wording while normalizing data for search, rules logic, and filtering.

## Non-Goals For Now

- Public commercial release.
- Supporting non-SRD or homebrew content before the SRD foundation is solid.
- Parsing the SRD PDF at runtime.
- Building campaign play before the core data model is reliable.

## Target Platforms

- iOS
- Android
- Web

## Distribution

The initial intended use is personal use only.

## Product Areas

### Compendium

Searchable reference for spells, loot, equipment, classes, subclasses, domains, ancestries, communities, adversaries, environments, and other SRD content.

### Character Builder

A guided step-by-step character creation flow based on structured SRD data and rules.

### Campaign Play

Playable campaign mode with character sheets, DM view, campaign membership, and real-time updates across clients.
